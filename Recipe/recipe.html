Chowmein

Ingredients

  • Sphagetti
  • Veggies
  • Sauces

Recipe

  1. Boil noodles and toss with oil to prevent sticking.
  2. Heat oil, sauté onions, and then stir-fry veggies on high heat.
  3. Add sauces, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Toss in the noodles and cook for 2–3 minutes. Serve hot.

Chilli Paneer

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Schezewan
  • Sauces

Recipe

  1. Coat paneer with cornflour, flour, salt, and pepper, then deep fry.
  2. Sauté ginger-garlic paste in oil, add onions and bell peppers.
  3. Add sauces and mix well.
  4. Toss in fried paneer and cook for a few minutes. Serve hot.

Manchurian

Ingredients

  • Manchurian Balls
  • Veggies
  • Sauces

Recipe

  1. Mix veggies with flour, shape into balls and deep fry.
  2. Sauté garlic in oil, add sauces, vinegar, and sugar.
  3. Add water, bring to boil, and toss in fried balls.
  4. Garnish with spring onion greens and serve hot.
